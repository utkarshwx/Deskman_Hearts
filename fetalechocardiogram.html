FETAL ECHOCARDIOGRAM

Monitor your Baby's Heart

Fetal echocardiography is a specialized ultrasound test that allows us to examine a developing baby's heart in the womb. By using advanced ultrasound technology, we can assess the structure and function of the baby's heart, identify potential rhythm-related issues and heart defects, and monitor their progression.

Early detection of heart conditions through fetal echocardiography enables timely intervention and treatment, improving the outcomes for affected babies. This non-invasive test is safe for both the mother and the baby and can provide parents with peace of mind.

About Us

Nurturing Young Hearts, One Beat at a Time

Welcome to The Upbeatheart Hearts Family. The Upbeatheart Hearts is an Initiative driven by the compassionate vision of Dr Soumya Kanti Mohapatra, a renowned Pediatric Cardiologist with over 16 years of experience in Paediatrics constituting of 8 years of specialised expertise in the field of Pediatric Cardiology. Dr. Mohapatra is currently a Senior Consultant, Pediatric Cardiology at Care Hospitals, Bhubaneswar. A journey from rural healthcare initiatives at Tata Steel Rural Development Society to comprehensive Pediatric care in Tata Main Hospital to acquiring skills in Pediatric Cardiology in Frontier Lifeline Hospital (Dr K M Cherian Heart Foundation), Chennai to implementing those skills in advanced pediatric cardiac centers in Kolkata like RTIICS, Medica Superspeciality Hospital then to Care Hospitals, Bhubaneswar, Dr Mohapatra is committed to providing comprehensive care to his patients.

The Upbeatheart Hearts Vision

To create a world where every heart is healthy by prioritizing preventive cardiology, advancing pediatric cardiac care, and empowering patients and their caregivers.
